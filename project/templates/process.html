How do we know that Magic8Ball is a successful design?

OVERVIEW

The need for Magic8ball emerged from three months of research with Bloomberg during our Spring semester. Based on the problems that we identified, creating a web app to help both Bloomberg and candidates with disabilities handle accommodation requests was important. Candidates with disabilities were not always sure if they should disclose, and recruiters an recruiting coordinators did not always know how to best handle an accommodation request.

However, how could we ensure that whatever we built would successfully address these problems? Our team used further research to both create a framework for measurement and test whether or not we were achieveing the results we wanted.

KEY EVALUATION METRICS

Ultimately, our team decision to measure the success of our designs using the following criteria for each group.

Candidates with Disabilities

Understand
Do I understand what is required for this stage of the interview?

Evaluate
Can I identify what I might need in terms of accommodation for this stage of the interview?

Confidence
Do I feel confident enough to request an accommodation?

Bloomberg Recruiting Coordinators

Efficiency
How easy is it for me to implement this accommodation request?

Confidence
Do I have the information I need to handle this accommodation?


SUCCESS FOR CANDIDATES WITH DISABILITIES

The pain point emerged from our research, that candidates don't always know if they need to disclose their disability. In order to understand how we could address this, we conduct several co-design sessions with people with disabilities. In one of these sessions, a participant shared a mental model that has served us well for evaluating our prototype. They focus on the following steps when trying to understand if they should request an accommodation:
Understand
What is expected of me?
Evaluate
For what might I need an accommodation?
Request
How can I easily ask for an accommodation?
The p trying to understand if they should request an accommodation:
Education
What will you be asked to do at this interview?
Evaluation
What accommodations might you need based on this information?
Confidence
How confident do you feel in requesting this accommodation?

We're thrilled to introduce Magic8Ball.
The tool that will allow people with and without disabilities to ensure they have exactly what they need during the hiring process! And that benefits both candidates with disabilities and Bloomberg.

For people with disabilities

  1. I know what to expect in the hiring process
  2. I can make smart decisions about what I need ahead of time
  3. I can confidently and easily request what I need

For Bloomberg

  1. I receive accommodation requests in advance
  2. I have help in learning about accommodations
  3. I have help in implementing any accommodations
