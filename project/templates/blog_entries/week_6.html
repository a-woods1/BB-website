Week 6
Racing Down Three Lanes
June X, 2018

Our team shifted to a rapid design, build, testing phase this week! Part of the change in our work has been to move into parallel lanes so we can move faster while checking in together.

Based on last week, we’re excited to have a few different things built out at this stage.

Research Lane

In addition to testing and collecting feedback, we also created a full taxonomy of accommodations--what the accommodation is, when in the hiring process it would be useful, and what it would take on the Bloomberg side to implement this accommodation (e.g. would the candidate need to supply medical documentation). This way, we have the content to properly inform our designs.

Design Lane

We created several iterations of a candidate’s potential journey through our tool, as well as low/medium fidelity designs for Bloomberg recruiters to manage and respond to accommodation requests.

Technology Lane

In addition to a prototype of our immersive room experience that is accessible via keyboard, we also created a cheat sheet for understanding VoiceOver, as screen reader accessibility will be a key component of our prototype for candidates with visual impairments.
